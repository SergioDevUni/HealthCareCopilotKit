# Famous Public Cases: How The App Could Help

These are teaching scenarios inspired by public reporting and historical sources. They are not medical reconstructions and should not be used to infer care decisions for the named people.

## Serena Williams: Postpartum Pulmonary Embolism Concern
- **What the app could help with**: keep postpartum status, recent surgery, prior clot history, and shortness of breath visible together; trigger emergency-level escalation; generate a concise handoff summary.
- **App feature demonstrated**: red-flag detection, case graph, visit-ready summary.
- **Sources**:
  - https://www.vogue.com/article/serena-williams-vogue-cover-interview-february-2018
  - https://www.health.harvard.edu/blog/if-pulmonary-embolism-can-strike-serena-williams-it-can-ace-anyone-201103031624

## Libby Zion: Medication Safety And Handoff Risk
- **What the app could help with**: force medication history into the structured graph, flag fever plus agitation/abnormal movements as urgent, and reduce lost context across handoffs.
- **App feature demonstrated**: missing-information radar, medication-history prompt, safety escalation.
- **Sources**:
  - https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK214940/
  - https://link.springer.com/article/10.1186/1472-6920-14-S1-S1

## John Snow And The Broad Street Cholera Outbreak
- **What the app could help with**: convert individual symptoms into a timeline/exposure pattern, reveal shared water exposure, and support public-health escalation.
- **App feature demonstrated**: timeline, exposure history, cluster thinking.
- **Sources**:
  - https://www.cdc.gov/mmwr/preview/mmwrhtml/mm5334a1.htm
  - https://journalofethics.ama-assn.org/article/lesson-john-snow-and-broad-street-pump/2009-06

## Bob Harper: Heart-Attack Awareness
- **What the app could help with**: prevent false reassurance based on high fitness, escalate chest-pressure/collapse language, and prompt for cardiac risk context and emergency response.
- **App feature demonstrated**: red-flag safety layer and emergency branch.
- **Sources**:
  - https://www.heart.org/en/health-topics/at-the-heart-of-it-with-nancy-brown/the-personal-side-of-fitness-with-bob-harper
  - https://newsroom.clevelandclinic.org/2017/03/09/nbc-nightly-news-reports-biggest-loser-trainer-bob-harper-following-heart-attack
